=====================================================
  TAWK.TO APOLLO AI SETUP GUIDE - ELITE WEALTH CAPITAL
=====================================================

Follow these steps to configure your AI Assistant:

-----------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: INSTRUCTIONS
-----------------------------------------------------
1. In Tawk.to dashboard, click "Instructions" tab
2. Open file: 1_INSTRUCTIONS.txt
3. Copy ALL the text (Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C)
4. Paste into the Instructions field
5. Click SAVE

-----------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: PERSONA
-----------------------------------------------------
1. Click "Persona" tab
2. Open file: 2_PERSONA.txt
3. Copy and paste the content into the appropriate fields:
   - Name: Elite Assistant
   - Role: Customer Support Representative
   - Set the greeting message
4. Click SAVE

-----------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: KNOWLEDGE (Text)
-----------------------------------------------------
1. Click "Knowledge" tab
2. Select "Text" option
3. Open file: 3_KNOWLEDGE_TEXT.txt
4. Copy ALL the text (Ctrl+A, Ctrl+C)
5. Paste into the text field (replace existing content)
6. Click SAVE
7. Make sure "Enabled" toggle is ON

-----------------------------------------------------
STEP 4: FAQs (Import CSV)
-----------------------------------------------------
1. Click "Knowledge" tab
2. Click "Add" button (top right)
3. Select "Import FAQs from CSV"
4. Upload the file: 4_FAQS.csv
5. Confirm the import

-----------------------------------------------------
STEP 5: TEST YOUR AI
-----------------------------------------------------
1. Click "Daily" tab on the left
2. Use the test chat to ask questions like:
   - "How do I invest?"
   - "What are the bank account details?"
   - "How do I withdraw?"
   - "What plans do you offer?"

-----------------------------------------------------
FILES INCLUDED:
-----------------------------------------------------
1_INSTRUCTIONS.txt  - AI behavior rules
2_PERSONA.txt       - AI personality settings
3_KNOWLEDGE_TEXT.txt - Full company knowledge base
4_FAQS.csv          - 15 FAQs ready for CSV import
README.txt          - This guide

=====================================================
  Your AI Assistant is now ready!
